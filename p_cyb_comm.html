Cybernetic Communications

EMF Sensing as Cybernetic Communications

The project narrates a transactional society of energy, where energy-harvesting and exchanging have become an essential part of living. When the intimacy between technology and humans turns the latter into cybernetic organisms, humans are partially mechanical, with no difference to systematically monitored entities, like farmed animals.

While physical proximities trigger overlaps in electromaqnetic fields (EMF) that enable energy-based interactions, the project imagines a juxtaposition of potential scenarios within the encounters between machines and humans.

The EMF-triggered haptic wearables illustrate the enhanced vision, where humans tune into strean of digital interactions and electromagnetic (EMF) vistas currentiv outside the human eyes
